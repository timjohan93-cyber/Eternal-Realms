V6.3 ADDED:
- Character equipment screen
- Mouse-based equipment slot interaction
- Inventory selection flow
- Safer single-press input handling

EQUIP FLOW:
1. Press I to open inventory.
2. Press item number to select an item.
3. Press K to open character screen.
4. Click the correct equipment slot.
5. Item equips if slot matches.

HOTKEYS:
1-4 = skills
Q = health potion
W = mana potion
I = inventory
K = character screen
E = equip selected shortcut
