V7.1 EQUIPMENT IDENTITY UPDATE

Implemented:
- MainV71.gd extends MainV69.gd.
- Main scene now uses MainV71.gd.
- Godlike item limit: maximum 2 equipped at the same time.
- Character HUD shows Godlike count.
- Equipment/Build screen shows Godlike Power Slots.
- Godlike aura visuals appear around the player when Godlike gear is equipped.
- 1 Godlike item = aura.
- 2 Godlike items = stronger aura + second orbiting orb.
- Weapon type foundation added through item normalization.
- Offhand type foundation added through item normalization.
- Rogue dual wield foundation:
  - Rogue can equip a one-handed weapon in the Offhand slot.
  - Rogue dual wield gives +15% attack speed and +10% crit chance.

Design intent:
- Godlike items should be powerful and visually special, but not dominate every gear slot.
- Limiting Godlike items to 2 creates build choices.
- Rings and amulets can feel visually important through aura effects even if they are not visible as models.
- Rogue dual wield creates a class identity and prepares the equipment system for future class-specific gear rules.
